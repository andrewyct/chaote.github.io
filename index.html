Bio

Chao-te Andrew Yang

Andrew (楊肇德) is a computer science graduate and a software developer with strong interest in web development and cloud computing. He has experience in Unix System-Level Programming, Object-Oriented Design, Java Enterprise Application Development, Android, Web Development with LAMP and MVC frameworks, Cloud-Based Application Development with MapReduce and AWS EC2/S3, and some knowledge of Front-End Development.

Andrew is a driven and self-motivated person, constantly learning new things. While he primarily focus on developing his technical aptitude, he also pursue a wide range of interests outside the the computer world. This personal website reflects his passion for technology, art, life, and photography. He holds a BS in Industrial Engineering from Yuan Ze University and an MS in Computer Science from Boston University.
